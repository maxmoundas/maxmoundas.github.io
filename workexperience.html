Specifically, I developed a Dynamic Distribution Group system that uses PowerShell scripts alongside SQL jobs to automate internal communications within the organization. This system was critical in ensuring that Aimbridge's tens of thousands of employees, including senior executives, were receiving critical messages on a regular basis.

One of the key benefits of this project was the discovery and elimination of over $150,000 in annual unnecessary Microsoft license fees. By streamlining the communication process, we were able to reduce the number of licenses required to support the system, resulting in substantial cost savings for the company.

This project was a major success, eliminating communication issues and ensuring that Aimbridge's associates receive internal communications when expected. Summer 2022

During my second summer internship with Aimbridge, I had the opportunity to work on a project that had a significant impact on the company's license management process. Specifically, I developed a system that automated the distribution of Microsoft Office 365 licenses to Aimbridge's 1,500 hotel properties using PowerShell scripts and organization of Aimbridge's Active Directory.

This system was critical in eliminating billing errors and ensuring that the accounting system between corporate offices and hotel properties was working properly. The new system streamlined the distribution process and ensured that bills were arriving at the correct properties, which has resulted in significant cost savings for the company.

By automating the distribution of Office 365 licenses, we were able to properly distribute over $2 million in annual fees, which had previously been a time-consuming and error-prone process. I am proud to have played a key role in this effort, and I believe that my experience with this project has helped me to develop a deep understanding of the importance of effective license management and organization.

This project was a major success, and I am excited about the prospect of continuing to find new and innovative ways to improve business processes and optimize systems through the use of technology.
